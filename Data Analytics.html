2018 Maine 2nd House Distrct Analysis

In this notebook I analyzed the 2018 race for Maine's second House District. Maine had adopted Instant Runoff Voting in 2016. 2018 was the first election cycle where it was used. The 2018 2nd House Race specifically was notable for the fact that it was the first election in US history to have had a different result due to IRV; that is to say had the rules not been changed, a different candidate would have been elected under First Past the Post.

In this analysis I broke down the totals each candidate had in each round and created a Sankey Diagram to visualize how many voters moved from each candidate to which candidate in each roound.
